Ingredients

Makes two servings.

  • 4 large eggs

  • 3 egg yolks
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • 1 teaspoon Dijon mustard
  • 1/2 cup of melted butter
  • Pinch of cayenne pepper
  • Salt and pepper to taste
