Ingredients

Makes two servings.

Poached Eggs:

  • 4 large eggs
  • 2 English muffins
  • 1 tablespoon white vinegar

Hollandaise Sauce:

  • 3 egg yolks
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • 1 teaspoon Dijon mustard
  • 1/2 cup of melted butter
  • Pinch of cayenne pepper
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt

Cooking Instructions

Hollandaise Sauce:

  1. Into a blender, add your egg yolks, dijon mustard, salt, and cayenne pepper. Blend on high for 5 seconds or until combined.
  2. Run the blender on medium while slowly streaming in the melted butter until the entire 1/2 cup has been added and the sauce has fully emulsified.
  3. Pour the hollandaise sauce into a covered serving dish to keep warm.

Poached Eggs:

  1. Fill a medium-sized pout halfway with water. Bring the water to a boil then reduce to a simmer.
  2. Add your white vinegar to the water.
  3. Stir the simmering water to make a vortex before carefully cracking an egg into the center of the vortex.
  4. Let the egg cook for 3-5 minutes depending on your desired level of yolk liquidity. Removed the poached egg from the water with a slotted spoon.
  5. Repeat steps 3 and 4 for the remaining eggs.

Assembly:

  1. Lightly toast the English muffin.
  2. Top each toasted English muffin half with a single poached egg, finishing with the desired amount of hollandaise sauce on top.
