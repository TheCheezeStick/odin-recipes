Ingredients

  • 1 large avocado
  • 2 slices preferred bread
  • 1 teaspoon lemon juice
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Cooking Instructions

  1. Toast two slices of your preferred bread and set aside on a plate.
  2. Slice the avocado in half and scoop the insides into a bowl. Discard the skin and pit.
  3. Add lemon juice and desired amount of salt and pepper to the bowl.
  4. Use a fork to press together and mix the ingredients. Mix until spread reaches desired level of smoothness.
  5. Evenly spread the avocado mixture onto each piece of toast.
