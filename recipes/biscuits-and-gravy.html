Ingredients

Makes 8 servings.

  • 1 (16 oz) can buttermilk biscuits
  • 1 (9.6 oz) package pork sausage
  • 1/4 cup flour
  • 2 1/2 cups milk
  • Salt and black pepper to taste

Cooking Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F. Arrange biscuits 1 to 2 inches apart on an ungreased cookie sheet. Bake for 13 to 15 minutes or until golden brown.
  2. Cook pork sausage in a large skillet over medium heat, stirring frequently until no pink remains.
  3. Add flour to sausage. Stir until well combined
  4. Raise the heat to high. Gradually add milk while continuously stirring until the gravy begins to thicken and comes to a boil. Reduce the heat to medium-low and allow to simmer while stirring for 2 more minutes. Season with salt and pepper to taste.

Assembly

  1. Split the biscuits in half and place two halves on a plate.
  2. Top the biscuit halves with desired amount of sausage gravy.
